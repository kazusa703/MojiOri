Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

MojiOri ("the App") is developed by an individual developer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All artwork data and history are stored locally on your device.

Photos

The App requests add-only access to your photo library solely to save generated artwork. We do not read or access your existing photos.

Advertising

The free version displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

Analytics

The App does not use third-party analytics services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store listing.
